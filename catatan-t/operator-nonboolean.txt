=========== Operator non boolean =========== 

Operator non bolean bukan lah operator yang menghasilkan true / false, namun menghasilkan output nilai dari truthy and falsy

Etc : 
      console.log("" || "Vincen");
      console.log(0 || "NOL");
      console.log(null || "NOL");
      console.log(undefined || "NOL");
      console.log(0 || false);

jika nilai null dapat menggunkaan 
nullish sytax object/variable ?? change result (default for cleasing)
